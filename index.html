About Us

Waghela Enterprises is a specialized infrastructure contractor dedicated to solving complex subsurface challenges. We deliver comprehensive excavation and tunnelling solutions ranging from conventional open-cut works to advanced trenchless technologies. Our experience with leading infrastructure developers demonstrates our capability to execute high-precision engineering works in challenging geological and urban environments.

Our Capabilities

NATM / SEM Tunnelling

Execution of underground tunnels in hard rock and mixed ground using Sequential Excavation Method with forepoling, SDA, lattice girders, wire mesh and M30 shotcrete support.

Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD)

Steerable trenchless solutions for river crossings, telecom ducts and long-distance pressure pipelines with minimal surface disruption.

Microtunneling (MTBM)

High-precision microtunneling for gravity sewers and critical utility crossings under highways and railways.

Pipe Jacking

Robust pipe jacking systems utilizing deep shafts and hydraulic jacking frames, adaptable to variable ground conditions.

Open Trench Excavation

High-productivity open-cut excavation for shallow utilities, foundations and box culverts with proper shoring and dewatering.

Logistics & Material Handling

In-house logistics ensuring timely supply of construction materials and large-diameter pipes to maintain project schedules.
